## CONTENT FORMATS (Week 1)

### FORMAT 1: Twitter Threads
- **Length:** 8-10 tweets (280 chars each)
- **Hook:** First tweet must stop scroll (question or stat)
- **Structure:** Intro → 3 points → Takeaway → CTA
- **CTA:** "Follow for more" or "Link in bio" (to Substack)
- **Time to write:** 30 minutes per thread
- **Post time:** 9:00 AM CT (when contractors wake up)

### FORMAT 2: Substack Blog Posts
- **Length:** 1,200-1,500 words
- **Structure:** Story intro → Problem → Solution → Implementation → Takeaway
- **Include:** 1-2 images/graphics if possible
- **CTA:** "Subscribe for more ideas"
- **Time to write:** 1-2 hours per post
- **Publish:** Same day as Twitter thread (morning)

### FORMAT 3: Video Scripts (Future)
- **Length:** 2-3 minutes
- **Tool:** Descript (auto-captions) or Synthesia (AI avatar)
- **Style:** Animated text + visuals, no face needed
- **Hook:** First 3 seconds must grab
- **Platform:** YouTube + TikTok (auto-uploaded)
- **Time to produce:** 2-3 hours per video

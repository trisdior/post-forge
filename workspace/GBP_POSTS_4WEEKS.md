**Body:**
People often ask: "How much will a kitchen remodel cost in Chicago?"

🔹 Budget Remodel: $15k-$30k (basic updates, cabinet refresh)

## 🎯 Pro Tips for GBP Success

1. **Timing:** Post on Monday mornings (8-10 AM) for max engagement
2. **Frequency:** Consistent weekly posting signals activity to Google (boosts local rankings)
3. **Photos:** Always include a high-quality photo (posts with images get 2-3x more engagement)
4. **Keywords:** Mention "Chicago" or your city naturally in posts (helps local search)
5. **CTA:** Every post should have a clear call-to-action (call, message, website link)
6. **Questions:** Occasionally ask a question in posts (increases comments = engagement boost)
7. **Mix content:** Don't make every post about selling; mix in helpful tips, customer stories, and company culture

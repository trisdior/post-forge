    Frequently Asked Questions

    How long does setup take?

    Usually 2-3 weeks from discovery to live. We do a full assessment of your business, build the custom AI, train you, and go live. You're in control the whole time.

    Will clients know they're talking to an AI?

    Not if you don't want them to. The AI responds in your voice, matches your brand, and handles conversations naturally. Most clients never know—they think it's your assistant.

    What if I need to make changes?

    You own everything. Change conversation flows, adjust pricing prompts, add new FAQs—all in your dashboard. Monthly coaching calls help you optimize based on real data.

    Can I cancel anytime?

    Yes. Month-to-month after your initial term. No contracts, no surprises. If Momentum isn't working, we'll refund your first month.

    Does it integrate with my existing tools?

    Most likely. We support Google Calendar, Calendly, Gmail, SMS, Zapier, and many CRMs. If you use something else, we can build a custom integration or find a workaround.

    What kind of business is this for?

    Anyone losing deals to poor follow-up or drowning in admin work. Contractors, real estate agents, auto shops, salons, restaurants, law firms, fitness studios, dentists—if you have leads, Momentum helps.

    How do I know it's working?

    Real-time dashboard shows leads, conversion rates, time saved, revenue impact. Monthly coaching calls review your metrics and refine the AI based on what's working.

    What if something breaks?

    We monitor 24/7. If something goes wrong, you're notified immediately and we fix it. Starter tier gets email support; Professional and above get priority/phone support.

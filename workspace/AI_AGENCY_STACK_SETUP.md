# AI Sales Agency — Client Setup Package

**Purpose:** Repeatable checklist for onboarding new contractors/small business clients to our AI assistant platform.

---

## 🎯 Phase 1: Discovery & Assessment (Week 1)

### Client Intake Interview
- [ ] Business type & industry
- [ ] Current pain points (top 3)
- [ ] Team size & structure
- [ ] Tech stack they're using (CRM, scheduling, etc.)
- [ ] Budget & ROI expectations
- [ ] Monthly lead/customer volume
- [ ] Most time-consuming admin tasks
- [ ] Available time for implementation

### Competitive Position
- [ ] What makes them different from competitors?
- [ ] Pricing model & margins
- [ ] Target customer profile
- [ ] Current marketing channels

---

## 🔧 Phase 2: AI Assistant Build (Week 1-2)

### Knowledge Base Assembly
- [ ] Company info (name, mission, services, service areas)
- [ ] Service menu & pricing (if public)
- [ ] Brand guidelines & voice
- [ ] Common objections & answers (from sales conversations)
- [ ] Case studies / portfolio (if applicable)
- [ ] Past customer feedback & testimonials
- [ ] FAQ from customer inquiries
- [ ] Email templates (proposals, follow-ups, etc.)
- [ ] Decision frameworks (qualification criteria, etc.)

### AI Systems Configuration
- [ ] **Lead Intake Assistant** — Auto-respond to form submissions / inquiries
  - Qualification questions
  - Schedule booking link
  - Instant response template
  
- [ ] **Email Follow-Up Sequences** — Auto-drafts after client interaction
  - Welcome sequence (3 emails over 7 days)
  - Proposal follow-ups (if no response in 48h)
  - Post-sale upsells
  
- [ ] **Social Media Content Generator** — Bulk create posts
  - Weekly posting calendar
  - Multi-channel templates (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Nextdoor)
  - Messaging framework
  
- [ ] **Customer Service Agent** — Handle common Q&A
  - FAQ chatbot style
  - Routing to human when needed
  
- [ ] **Analytics Dashboard** — Track performance
  - Lead volume & source
  - Response time metrics
  - Customer feedback scoring

### Integration Points
- [ ] Google Forms / website contact form → AI auto-response
- [ ] Calendar tool (Calendly, Google Calendar)
- [ ] Email platform (Gmail, Outlook)
- [ ] CRM if they have one (HubSpot, Jobber, Pipedrive, etc.)
- [ ] Social media accounts (Meta Business, LinkedIn, Nextdoor)

---

## 📊 Phase 3: Implementation & Training (Week 2-3)

### Setup & Testing
- [ ] All AI systems tested with real scenarios
- [ ] Response templates reviewed by client
- [ ] Integration tested (form → SMS/email delivery)
- [ ] Access credentials & logins documented

### Client Training
- [ ] Demo: How to edit prompts & responses
- [ ] Demo: How to update knowledge base
- [ ] Demo: How to monitor performance
- [ ] Demo: How to toggle features on/off
- [ ] Handoff: Documentation + support contact

### Launch Checklist
- [ ] Live on website/forms
- [ ] Social accounts connected
- [ ] Email sequences activated
- [ ] Client approval on all messaging
- [ ] 24/7 monitoring setup

---

## 📈 Phase 4: Optimization & Growth (Ongoing)

### Week 1 Review
- [ ] Lead volume metrics
- [ ] Response quality feedback
- [ ] Bottlenecks identified
- [ ] Adjustments made to prompts

### Monthly Ops
- [ ] Performance review call (conversion rate, response time, customer feedback)
- [ ] Update knowledge base (new services, pricing changes, testimonials)
- [ ] A/B test response templates
- [ ] Identify new workflows to automate
- [ ] Upsell opportunities (new modules, advanced features)

- Good for: Growing contractors, agencies with 1-2 people

### Tier 3: Enterprise ($1,299/month)
- Everything in Tier 2 +
- Unlimited CRM integrations

## 🚀 Scaling Notes

**Efficiency improvements:**
1. Create industry-specific templates (contractors, dentists, salons, etc.)
2. Build a "setup wizard" — templated questions that auto-generate knowledge base
3. Partner with web designers to embed AI form in their client sites
4. Create self-serve onboarding for technical clients (reduce setup support)
5. Develop "white-label" version for agencies to resell

**Upsell opportunities:**
- Content creation (branded videos, case studies)
- Lead generation campaigns (paid ads management)
- Reputation management (review monitoring)
- Custom integrations (Zapier, custom APIs)

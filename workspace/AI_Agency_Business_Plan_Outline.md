## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

We're building an AI-powered business automation service for solo operators and small teams who are drowning in admin work. We install an AI assistant (modeled on what we've built for Valencia Construction) that handles client outreach, follow-ups, lead qualification, scheduling, and operations—freeing the owner to focus on revenue-generating work.

**The Product:** Done-for-you AI agent setup + training + ongoing support  
**The Market:** Solo contractors, real estate agents, auto shops, restaurants, salons, law firms, medical offices, fitness studios, e-commerce shops, etc.  
**The Revenue Model:** Setup fee ($2k-5k) + monthly subscription ($500-2k depending on tier)  
**First Target:** Contractors in Chicago (we know the market, we have proof of concept)

---

## PART 1: PROBLEM STATEMENT

### The Pain Point

Solo operators and small business owners have a recurring problem:
- **Too many leads**, but poor follow-up system → lose deals
- **Admin work** eating 20-30% of their week (phone calls, emails, scheduling, proposals)
- **No consistent client communication** → poor retention, repeat business lost
- **Outdated tools** (spreadsheets, email chains, scattered systems)
- **Can't afford** a full-time admin person ($40-60k/yr + taxes + benefits)
- **Don't want to hire part-time** (complexity of training, supervision, turnover risk)

### The Gap

They've heard about AI, but:
- Don't know how to set it up
- Intimidated by the tech
- Don't want to pay $5k+ for a consultant who hands them something they don't understand
- Need it to integrate with their actual workflows, not just generic chatbots

---

## PART 2: THE SOLUTION

### What We Do

1. **Discovery Call** (1-2 hours)
   - Map their current workflows, pain points, lead sources
   - Identify what the AI should automate (lead follow-up? scheduling? proposal generation?)
   - Understand their communication style and business model

2. **AI System Design** (2-3 weeks)
   - Build custom AI assistant tailored to their business
   - Train it on their materials, pricing, FAQs, service offerings
   - Create conversation flows for common scenarios (lead inquiry, objection handling, scheduling, etc.)
   - Connect to their tools (calendar, CRM, email, SMS, etc.)

3. **Implementation & Training** (1-2 weeks)
   - Deploy the AI agent
   - Train owner + team on how to use it, monitor it, refine it
   - Document everything so they can maintain/adjust it

4. **Ongoing Support** (monthly)
   - Monitor performance, catch issues
   - Monthly coaching call to refine prompts, improve results
   - Help adjust as their business grows or needs change

### Why This Works (vs. Alternatives)

| | DIY AI | Generic Chatbot | Hire Admin | Our Service |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Setup complexity** | Very high | Moderate | Low | Low (we handle it) |
| **Customization** | High potential, hard to execute | Minimal | Full customization | High customization |
| **Cost** | Low $ upfront, high time cost | $200-500/mo, poor results | $40-60k/yr+ | $2k setup + $500-2k/mo |
| **Effectiveness** | Varies wildly | Poor (looks/acts like bot) | Good (but employee friction) | High (purpose-built) |
| **Scalability** | Hard to scale what you built | Same bot for everyone | Can't scale (1 person) | Scalable (multiple clients) |
| **Owner keeps control** | Yes, but complex | Limited | Limited | Yes |

## PART 4: REVENUE MODEL

### Tier Structure

| | **Starter** | **Professional** | **Enterprise** |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Setup Fee** | $2,000 | $4,000 | Custom ($5k+) |
| **Monthly Fee** | $500 | $1,200 | $2,500+ |
| **Included** | 1 AI agent, basic training, monthly check-in | 2 agents, advanced integrations, bi-weekly coaching | Custom build, multiple agents, dedicated support |
| **Lead Volume** | <100/mo | 100-500/mo | 500+/mo |
| **Support** | Email + monthly call | Priority support, bi-weekly calls | Dedicated success manager |
| **Best For** | Solo ops, testing | Growing businesses | Agencies, high-volume operators |

### Unit Economics (Starter Tier)

```
Setup Fee: $2,000
Monthly Revenue: $500/mo

Customer Lifetime Value (assuming 24 mo avg): 
$2,000 + ($500 × 24) = $14,000

Cost to Deliver (estimate):
- Initial setup & training: 12 hours @ $150/hr = $1,800
- Monthly support: 3 hours @ $150/hr = $450/mo
- Infrastructure/hosting: ~$50/mo
- Total first month cost: $2,300
- Monthly recurring cost: $500

Gross Margin (monthly): $500 - $500 = Break-even first month, then profitable

After 3 months: Customer lifetime margin = Positive
10 customers × $500/mo = $5,000/mo revenue
10 customers × ~$300/mo support/infra = $3,000/mo cost
Gross margin = $2,000/mo
```

**Profitability:** Break-even at 8-10 customers on Starter tier. High margin at scale.

## PART 5: GO-TO-MARKET STRATEGY

### Phase 1: Validation (Months 1-3)
**Goal:** Land 5-10 customers, prove ROI, refine the service

- **Target:** Chicago-area contractors we already know
- **Channels:** 
  - Direct outreach (LinkedIn, phone, email)
  - Facebook contractor groups
  - Nextdoor posts
  - Referrals from existing contacts
- **Offer:** Launch pricing ($300-400/mo for first 10 customers, lock in for 12 mo) to get testimonials + case studies
- **Success metric:** 10 customers with >50% lead increase or 30% time saved

### Phase 2: Proof & Positioning (Months 4-6)
**Goal:** Build case studies, refine messaging, expand to second market

- **Deliverables:**
  - 3-5 detailed case studies (before/after metrics)
  - Video testimonials from customers
  - Landing page + pricing page
  - Cold outreach templates tested
- **Channels:**
  - Contractors still (word-of-mouth, Google search)
  - Initial real estate agent outreach
  - Start building content (blog posts, YouTube shorts about AI for contractors)
- **Success metric:** 30+ customers, $15k/mo MRR

### Phase 3: Scale (Months 7-12)
**Goal:** 100+ customers, systematic sales funnel, hiring first support person

- **Products:**
  - Standardized package for contractors
  - Early versions for real estate + auto shops
  - Self-onboarding video series (reduce hands-on setup time)
- **Channels:**
  - Content marketing (blog, TikTok, YouTube, IG for contractors)
  - Partner with contractor networks
  - Affiliate partnerships (contractor software, business groups)
  - Paid ads (Google, Facebook) for contractors
- **Success metric:** 100+ customers, $50k/mo MRR, profitable

## PART 7: COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE

### Direct Competitors (AI Setup for Small Business)
- **ChatGPT + Zapier DIY** — Cheap but requires technical skill
- **HubSpot + automation** — Good but generic, expensive ($500+/mo)
- **Freshdesk/Zendesk** — Powerful but overkill for solo ops
- **Microsoft Copilot Studio** — New, powerful, but complex
- **Smaller AI agencies** — Custom builds but expensive ($10k+)

### Our Advantage
- **Pre-built, turnkey** — No technical barrier
- **Industry-specific** — Not generic automation
- **Affordable** — $500-2k/mo vs. $10k+ consultants
- **Ongoing support** — Not abandoned after setup
- **Proof of concept** — We built and use it (Valencia)

### Why We Win
We're not competing on being the most powerful AI platform. We're competing on being the easiest, fastest, and most affordable way for a contractor/real estate agent/shop owner to get an AI that actually works for their business.

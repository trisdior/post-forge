# AI Agency Client Onboarding Workflow
## How Valencia Delivers AI Assistant Services to Contractors

**Purpose:** Turn a signed contract into a fully operational AI assistant for a contractor within 14 days.
**Target Revenue:** $299-$999/month per client (recurring)
**Delivery Model:** 4-phase implementation

---

## 🎯 Phase 0: Pre-Sale (Discovery Call)

### Goal
Identify if the contractor is a good fit and understand their pain points.

### Activities
1. **30-min Discovery Call Script**
   - "What's eating up most of your time right now?"
   - "How many hours/week do you spend on admin, emails, follow-ups, quotes?"
   - "What would you do with 10 extra hours a week?"
   - "What's your biggest frustration with current tools?"

2. **Qualification Checklist**
   - ✅ Sole proprietor or small team (1-3 people)
   - ✅ Doing $50K-$500K/year in revenue
   - ✅ Spends >5 hrs/week on admin tasks
   - ✅ Has a basic website or social presence
   - ✅ Willing to adopt new tools

3. **ROI Pitch** (use these numbers)
   - If they save 8 hours/week at $75/hr billable rate = $600/week = $31,200/year in freed-up revenue
   - AI assistant costs $299-$599/month = $3,600-$7,200/year
   - **Net gain: $23,000-$27,600/year for $600-$200/month**
   - Payback period: 3-5 days

4. **Deliverable**
   - Send: Pre-filled proposal PDF via email
   - Link to: Video demo (2-min screencast of Valencia + AI in action)
   - CTA: "Let's get started. Click here to sign & setup."

---

## 📋 Phase 1: Setup & Data Transfer (Days 1-5)

### Goal
Get the contractor's business data into the system and configure the AI.

### Client Deliverables (Contractor provides)
1. **Company Info Sheet** (Google Form → Auto-fill template)
   - Business name, phone, service area, specialties
   - Website URL, social accounts
   - Current pricing/package structure
   - Top 3 pain points
   - List of 3-5 past clients (for testimonials)

2. **Business Documents** (Upload folder)
   - Current proposal/contract templates (PDF)
   - Invoice template
   - Standard email signatures
   - Any existing marketing materials
   - Logo files (high res + favicon)

3. **Customer Data** (CSV export)
   - Past client names, phone, email, project scope, date
   - Testimonials or review excerpts
   - Job cost breakdowns (to train cost estimator)

4. **Sales Collateral**
   - Photos: 3-5 best before/after project photos
   - Services list: What you offer + pricing
   - Service area map (zip codes covered)

### Valencia Setup Tasks
1. **Create AI Profile**
   - Name: "[Contractor Name] AI Assistant"
   - Personality: Brand voice + tone from provided materials
   - Knowledge base: All docs uploaded + indexed
   - Training data: Customer list, projects, pricing models

2. **Configure Automations**
   - Lead auto-response trigger (web form submission → SMS + email)
   - Appointment reminder text sequence
   - Customer feedback request (post-job completion)
   - Weekly lead summary email to contractor

3. **Build Custom Tools**
   - Cost estimator (trained on their historical jobs)
   - Availability checker (syncs with their calendar)
   - Lead qualifier (pre-screens bad leads)
   - Photo uploader (processes before/after images)

4. **Test Everything**
   - Send test leads
   - Verify SMS/email deliverability
   - Check AI response quality
   - Confirm calendar sync works

### Contractor Hands-On Time
~2 hours total (fill out forms, provide documents)

### Valencia Team Time
~8 hours (setup, config, testing, QA)

### Deliverable
- ✅ "You're Ready" email with:
  - Login credentials
  - Quick-start guide (1 page)
  - Video walkthrough (5 min)
  - Support contact info

---

## 🚀 Phase 2: Launch & Activation (Days 6-10)

### Goal
Get the AI live and answering real customer inquiries. Build contractor confidence.

### Activities

**Day 6: Go Live**
- Enable lead form on website (if they have one)
- Activate Telegram/WhatsApp connector for contractor to monitor AI
- Send test lead → confirm AI responds within 2 min
- Contractor sees it working in real-time

**Day 7-8: Train Contractor**
- 30-min live training session on:
  - How to review AI-generated responses
  - Editing/correcting responses (AI learns from corrections)
  - Overriding decisions (e.g., rejecting a bad lead)
  - Reviewing the daily lead summary
- Contractor fields 3-5 real leads with Valencia on standby
- Valencia coaches via Slack/text in real-time

**Day 9: Full Autonomy**
- Contractor manages leads solo
- Valencia monitors in background
- Contractor reports any issues/confusion

**Day 10: First Week Retrospective Call**
- How many leads came in?
- How many converted to actual inquiries?
- What worked well?
- What needs tweaking?
- Q&A on advanced features

### Deliverables
- Daily performance dashboard (leads → conversions → ROI)
- Recorded training video (for future reference)
- "First Week Report" (data + recommendations)

---

## 📈 Phase 3: Optimization & Scaling (Days 11-30)

### Goal
Dial in the AI's responses and expand across their channels.

### Activities

**Week 2: Response Tuning**
- Analyze all lead conversations
- Identify patterns where AI missed opportunities
- Rewrite/refine system prompts
- Test new response templates
- A/B test messaging variations

**Week 3: Channel Expansion**
- Add Facebook Messenger automation (if they have a page)
- Set up Google Business Profile auto-responses
- Create Nextdoor bot (local lead channel)
- Add SMS lead capture (text to number)
- Connect to their existing CRM (if applicable)

**Week 4: Content Automation** (Optional upsell)
- Set up weekly blog post generation
- Auto-post to social media (3x/week)
- Create monthly newsletter (AI-written, contractor-approved)
- Generate PDF reports (lead analysis, ROI dashboard)

### Metrics Tracked
- Leads per day (total + by source)
- AI response rate (how many inquiries it handled fully)
- Conversion rate (inquiry → actual bid request)
- Time saved (contractor's estimate)
- Revenue impact (new leads closed)

### Deliverables
- Monthly performance report (dashboard + narrative)
- Optimization recommendations (what to do next)
- ROI breakdown (cost vs. revenue generated)
- "Next steps" proposal (upsells: more channels, content automation, etc.)

## 💰 Upsell Opportunities (Month 2+)

Once the basic AI is running smoothly, offer:

1. **Content Automation** (+$199/mo)
   - Auto-generate blog posts + social content
   - AI writes, contractor approves & posts
   - 3-4 hours/week saved in content creation

2. **Video AI Assistant** (+$299/mo)
   - AI records personalized video responses to leads
   - "Thanks for reaching out, here's your custom quote..."
   - Dramatically increases conversion rates

3. **Appointment Automation** (+$149/mo)
   - AI books consultations directly into calendar
   - Sends automated confirmation + reminder texts
   - Integrates with Google Calendar / Calendly

4. **Review Aggregation & Monitoring** (+$99/mo)
   - Auto-collect reviews from Google, Facebook, Nextdoor, Yelp
   - Alert contractor to negative reviews instantly
   - Suggested responses for reviews

5. **Lead Intelligence Dashboard** (+$199/mo)
   - Real-time competitor price monitoring
   - Market trend analysis for their service area
   - Lead quality scoring (predict which leads will close)

6. **White-Label AI Resale** (custom pricing)
   - Contractor can resell AI to other contractors in their network
   - Valencia provides backend, contractor keeps 30% markup
   - Creates recurring revenue for contractor + Valencia

## Notes for Tris

- **This is the "operations manual" for selling AI to contractors.** Print it, live it, iterate on it.
- **Every section has a reason.** The workflow covers activation (trust-building), optimization (proves ROI), and expansion (recurring revenue).
- **Customize the timelines** as you learn what works. Maybe setup is faster. Maybe you need more training calls. Adjust based on real experience.
- **The metrics section** is what you report back to clients. Show them their ROI in black and white.
- **Upsells start early.** Don't wait until month 2 to mention content automation — hint at it in the Phase 3 retrospective call.
- **Track everything.** Every contractor you onboard should have a "Case Study" folder with their metrics, feedback, and results. These become your best sales tools.

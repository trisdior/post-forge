# Automated Social Media Posting System for Valencia Construction

## The Problem
Manually posting 2x daily (8am + 8pm) = 14 posts/week = 728 posts/year = sustainable, but boring.
**Solution:** Schedule posts in advance. Write 4 weeks of content once, let automation run.

---

## Platform Strategy

### Platform 1: Facebook (Highest ROI for Local Contractors)
- **Audience:** Chicago homeowners, property managers, realtors
- **Posting frequency:** 2x daily (8 AM + 5 PM)
- **Content:** Mix: before/afters, tips, customer stories, testimonials, local events
- **Tool:** Meta Business Suite (FREE, native scheduling)

### Platform 2: Google Business Profile
- **Audience:** People searching for "contractors near me"
- **Posting frequency:** 3x per week (Wed, Fri, Sun)
- **Content:** Project highlights, seasonal tips, promotions
- **Tool:** Google Business Profile native scheduler

### Platform 3: Instagram (Future - wait for real before/afters)
- **Holding pattern** — wait until you have actual project photos

### Platform 4: Nextdoor (Neighborhood-specific, HIGH conversion)
- **Audience:** Hyperlocal Chicago homeowners
- **Posting frequency:** 2x per week
- **Content:** Local expertise, availability, quick win stories
- **Tool:** Nextdoor native posting + scheduling

## Setup Instructions

### FACEBOOK BUSINESS SUITE (RECOMMENDED START)

#### Step 1: Link Facebook Page to Business Suite
1. Go to [Business Suite](https://business.facebook.com)
2. Select your Valencia Construction page
3. Go to **Content Calendar**
4. You can now schedule posts up to 75 days in advance

#### Step 2: Schedule a Post
1. Click **Create Post**
2. Write content
3. Click **Schedule Post** (bottom right)
4. Pick date + time (8 AM or 5 PM)
5. Publish

#### Template Schedule (Sample 2-Week Rotation):

```
MONDAY
8 AM:  "Monday Motivation" — before/after from past project
5 PM:  Customer story ("Here's why Juan chose Valencia...")

TUESDAY
8 AM:  "Tip Tuesday" — bathroom/kitchen/flooring tip
5 PM:  Local event or seasonal hook

WEDNESDAY
8 AM:  "What's possible in Chicago?" — market tip
5 PM:  Team photo or office update

THURSDAY
8 AM:  "Project of the Week" — past job highlight
5 PM:  Q&A or FAQ ("Does remodeling add value?")

FRIDAY
8 AM:  "Friday Wins" — completed project showcase
5 PM:  Promo or availability announcement

SATURDAY
8 AM:  "Saturday Series" — design inspo / product spotlight
5 PM:  Educational content (YouTube video, blog link)

SUNDAY
8 AM:  "Sunday Plans?" — encourage people thinking about remodels
5 PM:  Review request or success story
```

---

### GOOGLE BUSINESS PROFILE POSTING

#### Setup:
1. Go to [Google Business Profile](https://business.google.com)
2. Select Valencia Construction
3. Navigate to **Posts**
4. Click **New Post**
5. Choose post type: **Update, Event, Product, or Offer**
6. Schedule up to 30 days out

#### 3x Per Week Schedule:
- **Wednesday 10 AM:** Before/after or project showcase
- **Friday 2 PM:** Seasonal tip or promotional
- **Sunday 6 PM:** Local event or availability

---

### NEXTDOOR POSTING

#### Setup:
1. Go to [Nextdoor.com](https://nextdoor.com)
2. Switch to "Business" mode (if not already)
3. Click **Compose**
4. Write message + upload photo if available
5. Nextdoor doesn't have scheduling, but you can batch-write posts

#### 2x Per Week (Manual, but quick):
- **Monday evening:** Introduce yourself / availability
- **Thursday morning:** Success story or local tip

## AUTOMATION TOOLS (FUTURE EXPANSION)

Once you have consistent traffic & leads, upgrade to these:

### Buffer / Later / Hootsuite
- Schedule posts across 5+ platforms at once
- Best for: Multi-platform growth (TikTok, YouTube Shorts, LinkedIn)
- Cost: $15-99/month
- **ROI:** Save 2 hours/week = $104/week if your hourly rate is $50+

### AI Content Generation (OPTIONAL)
- Tools like Jasper, Copy.ai, or ChatGPT can generate post copy
- **Workflow:** Write outline → AI drafts → You edit → Schedule
- **Time:** 3 posts in 10 minutes instead of 30
- Cost: $20-50/month
- **ROI:** Worth it once you have the process locked

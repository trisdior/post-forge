# Valencia Construction — Daily Lead Gen System

## 🌅 Morning Routine (9:00 AM — 30 min)

### 1. Check Alerts (5 min)
- [ ] Check Telegram for Craigslist lead alerts from Steve
- [ ] Check Gmail for form submissions
- [ ] Check Google Business Profile for messages

### 2. Facebook Groups (15 min)
Post or reply in 3 groups. Copy/paste templates below.

**Groups to join & monitor:**
- Chicago Home Improvement & Remodeling
- Chicago Handyman & Contractor Services
- Chicago Friendly Landlord Group
- Chicago Real Estate Investors
- Chicago Homeowners Network
- Nextdoor (your neighborhood + nearby)

**Reply template (when someone asks for contractor):**
> Hey! I run Valencia Construction — licensed & insured, based right here in Chicago. We do [their specific project]. Happy to come take a look and give you a free estimate. DM me or call/text (773) 682-7788 🔧

**Proactive post template (post 2-3x/week):**
> 🔧 Licensed & insured Chicago contractor here! We handle kitchen remodels, bathroom renovations, flooring, painting, drywall, plumbing — you name it. Owner-operated, transparent pricing, and we actually show up when we say we will. Free estimates — call/text (773) 682-7788 or visit valenciaconstructionchi.com

### 3. Make 5 Cold Calls (10 min)
Work through the small PM list (small-pm-targets.md). 5 calls/day = all 20 done in 4 days.

**Cold call script:**
> "Hi [Name], this is Tris from Valencia Construction. We're a licensed and insured contracting company here in Chicago. I'm reaching out to local property managers because we specialize in the kind of work you need — unit turnovers, maintenance, renovations. We're reliable, our pricing is upfront, and you deal directly with me, the owner. I'd love to earn your business — could I swing by one of your properties for a free estimate on any upcoming work?"

**If voicemail:**
> "Hi [Name], Tris from Valencia Construction, (773) 682-7788. Licensed and insured Chicago contractor — we do turnovers, remodels, maintenance, the works. Would love to be your go-to guy. Give me a call back or text anytime. Thanks!"

**Follow-up text after call (send immediately):**
> Hi [Name]! This is Tris from Valencia Construction — just called about working together on your properties. Licensed & insured, free estimates, and I always pick up the phone. Here's our site: valenciaconstructionchi.com. Let me know if you have anything coming up! 🔧

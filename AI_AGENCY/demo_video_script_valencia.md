# Valencia Construction AI Demo Video Script

**Format:** 3-4 minute YouTube/LinkedIn video showing what the AI system does
**Audience:** Other contractors (B2B sales)
**Goal:** Show that this works for real contractors, not just theory
**Tone:** Professional, straightforward, emphasize the business impact (not tech)

### Scene 3: Here's What Changed (1:00-1:45)

**VISUAL:** Show the AI in action (screen recording or animated walkthrough):

**Step 1: Client Inquiry Arrives (via email/form/text)**

**Step 2: AI Responds Immediately (within 30 seconds)**

**Step 5: Appointment Happens**
- Tris talks to client in person
- Knows everything upfront (no time wasted on basics)
- Closer sale because client sees responsiveness

It answers their basic questions, books an appointment in our calendar, and gets them any info they need. We get one clean summary delivered to us. No back-and-forth. No missed details. Just qualified leads ready to talk."

## Voiceover Direction

- **Tone:** Confident, straightforward, not salesy
- **Speed:** Measured. Let people absorb the info.
- **Emphasis:** Business impact first (more leads, more money, less stress). Tech is secondary.
- **Language:** Avoid jargon. "AI system" is fine. Avoid "machine learning," "natural language processing," "API integrations."

## Video Production Notes

**DIY Option (Quick):**
- Screen recording + voiceover + simple text overlays
- Use free tools: OBS (recording), Canva (graphics), Audacity (voiceover)
- Can be produced in 1-2 hours
- Will look "founder authentic" not polished (which is fine)

**Professional Option:**
- Hire a video editor ($500-1500)
- Professional voiceover ($100-200)
- B-roll of actual Valencia projects
- Polished look

**Recommendation:** Start with DIY. It's fast and authentic. If sales calls require it, upgrade to professional.

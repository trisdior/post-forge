## Personalization Checklist

Before sending any template:
- [ ] Use their actual name (not "Hey there")
- [ ] Mention their company by name
- [ ] Reference something specific about their business (# of staff, pain point you observe, mutual connection)
- [ ] Customize the numbers (how much time they're probably losing, what their close rate might be)
- [ ] Include your phone number — makes it feel less like spam
- [ ] One template per person (don't send multiples)
- [ ] Space out sends (not 10 in one day)

## Success Metrics

Track which templates perform best:
- Open rate (email)
- Reply rate
- Meeting booked rate

Invest more in templates that convert.

**Our goal:** 5-10 contractors interested → 3 sales conversations → 1-2 new clients per month

---

## Red Flags (Don't Reach Out)

Skip contractors/businesses that:
- Already have a full-time admin person (they don't need this)
- Too small (sole operator probably manages fine)
- Too large (probably already have systems in place)
- In industries that don't take cold outreach well
- Where you have no mutual connection or referral angle

Focus on the sweet spot: 5-15 person teams where the owner is stretched thin.

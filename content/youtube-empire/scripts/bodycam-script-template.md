## WRITING TIPS
- **Don't over-narrate.** The footage is the star. You're the guide, not the main attraction.
- **Let tense moments breathe.** When something crazy happens, SHUT UP and let it play.
- **Be factual, not sensational.** State what happened. Don't say "INSANE" or "CRAZY" in the script (save that for titles/thumbnails).
- **Always note legal context.** Viewers love knowing if something was legal or not.
- **End with a question.** Drives comments, which drives algorithm.

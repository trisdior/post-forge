## Growth Strategy
1. Put Discord link in every video description + pinned comment
2. Run Discord-exclusive polls ("vote on the next video")
3. Give early access to videos for Discord members (upload unlisted, share link)
4. Monthly giveaways for active members (merch, gift cards)
5. Use `#video-suggestions` and `#footage-submissions` as free research labor

# Google Business Profile Optimization Checklist — Valencia Construction

## ✅ Profile Setup (Do These First)

- [ ] **Business name:** "Valencia Construction" (exact legal name, no keyword stuffing)
- [ ] **Primary category:** "General Contractor"
- [ ] **Secondary categories:** "Kitchen Remodeler", "Bathroom Remodeler", "Home Improvement Contractor"
- [ ] **Service area:** Chicago + suburbs you're willing to travel to (set specific cities/zip codes, not just "Chicago area")
- [ ] **Phone number:** Primary business number (the one you always answer)
- [ ] **Website:** valenciaconstruction.com
- [ ] **Business hours:** Set realistic hours (e.g., Mon–Fri 7am–6pm, Sat 8am–2pm)
- [ ] **Business description:** Use all 750 characters. Include "Chicago", your services, and what makes you different. No ALL CAPS, no phone number in description.

### Suggested Business Description:
> Valencia Construction is a Chicago-based general contracting company specializing in residential remodeling. We handle kitchen remodels, bathroom renovations, basement finishing, flooring, painting, and full-home renovations across Chicago and the surrounding suburbs. As a locally owned business, we take pride in transparent pricing, clear communication, and quality craftsmanship. Contact us for a free estimate.

## ✅ Photos (Critical for Clicks)

- [ ] **Logo:** Clean, high-res square logo
- [ ] **Cover photo:** Best project photo (bright, well-lit, finished result)
- [ ] **Interior photos:** 5–10 photos of completed projects (kitchens, bathrooms, etc.)
- [ ] **Team photo:** Even a photo of you at a job site builds trust
- [ ] **Before/after sets:** These perform incredibly well — post pairs side by side

**Photo tips:**
- Minimum 720px wide
- Well-lit, no filters
- Show finished results, not mid-construction chaos
- Add new photos monthly (Google rewards active profiles)

## ✅ Reviews Strategy

- [ ] Set up a direct review link (GBP dashboard → "Ask for reviews" → copy link)
- [ ] Save the link somewhere easy to text to clients
- [ ] Ask for reviews within 48 hours of project completion
- [ ] Respond to EVERY review (positive and negative) within 24 hours
- [ ] Never offer incentives for reviews (violates Google policy)

**Review response templates:**

*Positive review:*
> Thank you [Name]! It was a pleasure working on your [project type]. We appreciate you trusting Valencia Construction with your home. If you ever need anything in the future, don't hesitate to reach out!

*Negative review:*
> Hi [Name], thank you for your feedback. We take every concern seriously. I'd like to discuss this further and make it right — please call me directly at [phone]. We're committed to delivering quality work and want to resolve this.

## ✅ Posts (Weekly)

Google Business Profile posts show up in search results and Maps. Post weekly.
- **Update posts:** Expire after 7 days, use for promotions/news
- **Event posts:** For open houses, community events
- **Offer posts:** Special promotions with start/end dates

- **Search queries:** What terms people find you for

- **Photo views:** Compared to competitors
- **Review count and rating:** Aim for 4.5+ stars, 10+ reviews in first 3 months

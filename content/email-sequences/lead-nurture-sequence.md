## Implementation Notes

**How to automate this:**
1. **Mailchimp** (free up to 500 contacts) — create two automation sequences
2. **Brevo (formerly Sendinblue)** — free plan includes automations, better for transactional
3. **MailerLite** — free up to 1,000 subs, great automation builder

**Recommended setup:**
- Use Brevo or MailerLite (both free tier, both support automations)
- Connect your website contact form to the email tool via Zapier (free tier) or direct integration
- Tag leads as "new-inquiry" or "estimate-sent" to trigger the right sequence
- Personalize [First Name], [project type], etc. with merge fields

**Important:**
- Include an unsubscribe link in every email (legally required)
- Don't add people to the list without their consent (CAN-SPAM)
- Keep the "from" address consistent
- Set up a professional email: tris@valenciaconstruction.com

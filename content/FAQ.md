# Valencia Construction FAQ

## About Us & Our Process

### How long has Valencia Construction been in business?
Valencia Construction is a Chicago-based remodeling company with years of experience transforming homes throughout the Chicagoland area. We specialize in kitchens, bathrooms, and full-home renovations with a focus on modern design and quality craftsmanship.

### Do you work in my area?
We primarily serve the Chicago metropolitan area, including downtown Chicago, North Shore, Northwest suburbs, West suburbs, and South suburbs. If you're unsure whether your location is in our service area, contact us — we may be able to accommodate you.

### How do I get started?
1. **Contact us** via our website form, phone, or message
2. **Free consultation** — We meet at your home to discuss your vision
3. **Detailed estimate** — You'll receive a professional proposal within 5-7 business days
4. **Sign & schedule** — Once you approve, we schedule your project
5. **Transformation begins** — Our team handles everything from permits to final walkthrough

### What if I'm not ready to start immediately?
That's fine! We can provide an estimate now, and you can reach out when you're ready to begin. We typically book 2-3 months in advance, so planning ahead helps.

## Pricing & Estimates

### How much does a kitchen remodel cost?
Kitchen remodels typically range from **$20,000–$80,000+** depending on size, materials, and scope. A small galley kitchen with updated cabinets might be $25,000, while a full luxury kitchen with custom cabinetry and high-end appliances could exceed $100,000. We'll give you a precise number after our consultation.

### How much does a bathroom remodel cost?
Bathroom remodels typically range from **$8,000–$40,000+**. A basic update with new tile and fixtures might be $12,000, while a spa-like master bath with custom tile work and heated floors could be $35,000+. Again, we'll know more after we see your space.

### How do you price your work?
We provide **transparent, itemized estimates** that break down:
- Materials (cabinets, tile, counters, fixtures, paint, etc.)
- Labor (installation, carpentry, plumbing, electrical, etc.)
- Permits & inspections
- Timeline

No hidden fees. What you see is what you pay.

### Can you work within a specific budget?
Absolutely. Tell us your budget, and we'll design a scope that makes sense. We can prioritize high-impact upgrades and suggest ways to maximize value.

### What if costs go over budget?
We'll notify you immediately and discuss options. Change orders are documented in writing before work continues. Surprise costs aren't our style.

### Do you offer financing?
We can discuss financing options and may be able to recommend partners. Ask during your consultation.

## The Renovation Process

### How long does a project take?
Timeline varies by scope:
- **Kitchen remodel:** 4–8 weeks
- **Bathroom remodel:** 2–4 weeks
- **Full home renovation:** 3–6 months
- **Partial remodel (1-2 rooms):** 6–10 weeks

We'll give you a specific timeline after we assess your project.

### Will the work disrupt my daily life?
We're thoughtful about minimizing disruption. We establish a work schedule with you upfront. Kitchens and bathrooms obviously require some inconvenience, but we can sometimes arrange phased work or temporary solutions (like a temporary kitchen setup) for longer projects.

### How do you handle permits?
We handle all permit applications and inspections. We're licensed and insured, and we follow all Chicago building codes. You don't have to worry about this — we do.

### What if you find problems (like mold or structural issues)?
We'll stop work, document the issue, and discuss options with you immediately. We'll provide a separate estimate for fixing hidden problems so there are no surprises.

### Can I change things mid-project?
Changes can be made, but they may affect timeline and cost. Any changes are documented as change orders in writing. Small tweaks are usually fine; major redesigns midway through can cause delays.

### What warranty do you offer?
We guarantee our work. Any defects in craftsmanship are corrected at no cost. Material warranties come directly from manufacturers. We're standing behind our work — if something isn't right, we fix it.

## Payment & Contracts

### How do you structure payments?
Typically we ask for:
- **30%** upon signing to order materials
- **30%** mid-project as work progresses
- **40%** upon completion and final walkthrough

We'll discuss the specific payment schedule for your project.

### What payment methods do you accept?
Cash, check, credit card, and ACH transfers. We'll confirm payment methods during the contract phase.

### Do I need a contract?
Absolutely. Everything is in writing: scope, timeline, cost, payment schedule, and warranty. It protects both of us.

### What if I need to pause or cancel?
We'll discuss this upfront. If work hasn't started, cancellation is straightforward. If work is in progress, you're responsible for materials already purchased and labor completed to that point.

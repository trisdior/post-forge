## Day 13 — Sunday, Mar 1
**Type:** Value Post | **Groups:** Homeowner

**Flooring 101: LVP vs. Hardwood vs. Tile**

🟫 **Hardwood** — Beautiful, adds home value, but expensive and hates moisture. Not ideal for kitchens/baths.

🟦 **LVP (Luxury Vinyl Plank)** — Waterproof, durable, looks great, and half the cost of hardwood. Best bang for your buck in most rooms.

🟨 **Tile** — King of bathrooms and kitchens. Lasts forever. More labor-intensive to install but worth it in wet areas.

My recommendation for most homeowners? LVP in living areas, tile in bathrooms. Saves money and looks sharp.

## Day 26 — Saturday, Mar 14
**Type:** Value Post | **Groups:** Homeowner

**Paint finish guide — which sheen for which room:**

- **Flat/Matte** — Ceilings only. Hides imperfections but impossible to clean.
- **Eggshell** — Living rooms, bedrooms. Slight sheen, easy to touch up.
- **Satin** — Kitchens, hallways, kids' rooms. Wipeable and durable.
- **Semi-Gloss** — Bathrooms, trim, doors. Moisture resistant, easy to clean.
- **High Gloss** — Accent pieces only. Shows every imperfection but looks striking.

The right finish matters as much as the right color. Your painter should know this.

— Tris, Valencia Construction

## STEP 6: Process a Transaction

**When customer is ready:**

1. Customer applies via your Affirm link
2. Affirm approves (instant or next day)
3. Affirm pays you 97.1% (minus 2.9% fee)
4. You get $ in bank in 1-2 days
5. Customer pays Affirm over 12 months

**Example:**
- Project cost: $25,000
- Fee (Affirm's cut): $725
- You receive: $24,275 (immediately)
- Customer pays Affirm: $208/month × 12 = $2,496 total interest (0% APR = no interest, just split)

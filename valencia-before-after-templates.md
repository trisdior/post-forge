# Valencia Construction — Before/After Project Templates

## Why This Matters

Before/after photos are your most powerful sales tool. But the description is what sells it.

A bad caption: "Kitchen remodel. Before and after."
A great caption: "Kitchen remodeling from outdated to stunning. Custom cabinets, new appliances, porcelain tile backsplash. Client: 'Best investment we've made in our home.' Timeline: 6 weeks. Cost: $38K."

**The formula:** [What was wrong] → [What we fixed] → [Materials/finishes] → [Social proof] → [Timeline & cost]

## Photography Guidelines (When You Get Real Projects)

To make before/afters powerful, photograph like this:

### BEFORE Shots
- Harsh lighting (shows every flaw)
- Wide angle (shows full scope)
- Same angle as AFTER shot
- Include context (you can see how ugly it was)

### AFTER Shots
- Professional lighting (warm, flattering)
- Same angle as BEFORE
- Tight shot (highlight new details)
- Lifestyle shot (person in space, if possible)

### Best Platforms for Before/Afters
1. **Instagram** — 3-4 before/after carousel posts per month
2. **Facebook** — Post to Valencia Construction page + local groups
3. **Google Business Profile** — Every project uploaded here for local search
4. **Website Portfolio** — Create dedicated gallery page
5. **Nextdoor** — Neighborhood-specific (high conversion)

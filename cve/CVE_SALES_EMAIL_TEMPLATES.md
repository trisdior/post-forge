## Send Strategy

### Cold List Approach
1. Send Template 1 (Cold Outreach) to 20 contractors
2. Wait 1 week
3. Send Template 6 (Follow-up) to non-responders
4. Wait 2 weeks
5. Send Template 5 (Objection-based) to non-responders
6. Move those to "later" list
7. Repeat with next 20

### Warm List Approach (Referrals)
1. Send Template 3 (Referral-based) immediately
2. Follow up with Template 6 if no response after 1 week
3. Higher conversion rate (warm intro = 10-15% conversion vs 2-3% cold)

### Current Customer Approach
1. Send Template 9 (Case Study) after 30 days with early customer
2. Use real numbers and testimonial
3. Send to contractors similar to that customer (same service area or service type)
4. 30-40% conversion (warm social proof)

### Timing
- **Best times to send:** Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM (people checking inbox)
- **Avoid:** Monday (busy), Friday (checking out), evenings (low open rate)
- **Follow-ups:** 5-7 days after initial (high response rate)

### Personalization Checklist
Before sending, replace:
- [ ] [Contractor Name] — their actual name
- [ ] [Service] — their specialty (kitchen, bathroom, flooring, etc.)
- [ ] [Referrer] — whoever referred them
- [ ] [City] — their city/area
- [ ] [Link] — your actual calendar link
- [ ] [Phone] — your phone number
- [ ] [Email] — your email

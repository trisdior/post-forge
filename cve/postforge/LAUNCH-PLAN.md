# PostPilot AI — Launch Plan

## Product
AI social media content generator for small businesses.
Generates 30 days of platform-specific posts in 60 seconds.

## Pricing
- **$29/mo** subscription (7-day free trial)
- 66 customers = $1,914 MRR (target: $1,900)

## Revenue to $1,900

### Week 1: Soft Launch
1. Deploy to Vercel (free tier)
2. Add Stripe Checkout ($29/mo subscription)
3. Post in Reddit threads where pain was expressed:
   - r/smallbusiness — "Lost clients because Instagram inactive"
   - r/Entrepreneur — content creation pain threads
   - r/marketing — social media management threads
4. Post on X with #BuildInPublic hashtag
5. Submit to ProductHunt

### Week 2: Growth
1. Cold DM small businesses with inactive Instagram pages
2. Run $50 Facebook ad targeting small business owners
3. Post results/case studies on Reddit
4. Create YouTube short showing the tool in action

### Week 3-4: Scale
1. Add referral program (give 1 month, get 1 month)
2. Partner with business coaches/consultants
3. List on AppSumo for one-time deal ($49 lifetime = quick cash)
4. Target specific niches (contractors, realtors, restaurants)

- **Price**: $29/mo vs $1,500/mo for a social media manager

- **Simple**: No complex dashboards. Generate → Review → Schedule.

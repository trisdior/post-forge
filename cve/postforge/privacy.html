Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Email addressAccount creation, login, support

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the PostForge service
  • To generate personalized social media content for your business
  • To manage your account and subscription
  • To send important service updates (no spam, ever)

3. What We Don't Do

  • We never sell your data to third parties
  • We never use your business data to train AI models
  • We never send unsolicited marketing emails
  • We never share your information with advertisers

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — to generate content. Your business name, industry, and location are sent to generate posts. Anthropic does not use API inputs for training.
  • Stripe — to process payments securely. We never see or store your full card number.
  • Vercel — to host the application.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely and encrypted. Passwords are hashed using PBKDF2 with unique salts. We use HTTPS for all connections. We retain your data only as long as your account is active.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your data at any time
  • Delete: Request full account and data deletion
  • Export: Download your generated content as CSV
  • Correct: Update your information through your account

7. Cookies

We use only essential cookies (session tokens stored in localStorage). No tracking cookies, no analytics cookies, no third-party cookies.

8. Children's Privacy

PostForge is not intended for use by anyone under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our service evolves. We'll notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.

10. Contact

Privacy questions? Contact us at privacy@postforge.com.

Source: r/smallbusiness "Exploring an Affordable, Hands-Off Social Media Posting Service"

**Lesson:** They don't want to learn tools. They want it DONE. PostForge should feel like hiring someone, not using software.

Source: r/smallbusiness "raised prices by 40%, lost 30% of clients"

**Lesson:** Our $9/mo Pro tier is a massive advantage. Stay cheap.

### 5. AI Content Gets Flagged as "AI Slop"

**Lesson:** PostForge MUST generate content that sounds human, not robotic. Brand voice customization is critical.

## Competitor Review Weaknesses to Exploit

### Hootsuite
- Too expensive ($99/mo minimum)
- Complex UI — small businesses don't need 50 features
- No AI content generation

### Buffer
- Basic — no AI generation
- Per-channel pricing adds up fast
- Limited analytics on free tier

### Predis.ai
- $32/mo minimum for AI content
- Interface is cluttered
- Generated content often generic
- No industry-specific templates

### Opus Clip
- $19/mo minimum
- Quality inconsistent
- Can't handle all video formats
- No social media posting integration

### Later / Planoly
- Visual-first but no AI generation
- Instagram-focused, weak on other platforms
- Pricing scales badly for multi-platform

# PostPilot AI — Pricing Tiers

## Free Tier
- 5 posts per month (per platform)
- 1 platform (Instagram OR Facebook OR X)
- Basic templates only
- PostPilot watermark on exports
- No scheduling
- Community support

## Pro — $9/mo
- 30 posts per month per platform
- All platforms (Instagram, Facebook, X, LinkedIn, TikTok)
- All content types (tips, showcase, engagement, promo, testimonial)
- Hashtag optimization
- Image prompt suggestions
- Export as CSV / calendar
- Email support
- Remove watermark

## Business — $19/mo
- Unlimited posts
- All Pro features
- Multiple businesses / brands
- Custom brand voice training
- Content calendar with scheduling
- Analytics dashboard
- Priority support
- Beta features access

## Revenue Math
- Free → Pro conversion: ~5-10%
- Pro → Business conversion: ~20%
- Target: 1,000 free users → 75 Pro ($675) → 15 Business ($285) = $960/mo from first 1,000 users
- Scale to 5,000 free users → $4,800/mo
- Scale to 10,000 free users = ~$9,600/mo

## Beta Features (Business tier only)
- AI image generation for posts
- Auto-scheduling to platforms
- Competitor content analysis
- Trending content suggestions
- Multi-language support
- Team collaboration
- White-label for agencies

## What NOT to Do

- ❌ Don't spam. Be genuinely helpful.
- ❌ Don't promise features you don't have.
- ❌ Don't ignore early user feedback.
- ❌ Don't launch with bugs (test everything).
- ❌ Don't neglect support (respond to all DMs/comments).
- ❌ Don't burn cash on ads yet (organic first).

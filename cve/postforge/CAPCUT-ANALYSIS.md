# CapCut Competitive Analysis

## What CapCut Does Well
- Video editing (their core) — best free video editor on mobile
- Templates (44+ mentions on homepage)
- AI features (151 mentions): auto captions, background removal, effects
- TikTok integration (owned by ByteDance = same parent)
- Free tier is genuinely usable

## CapCut Weaknesses (From Reddit)
1. **ENSHITTIFICATION** — r/VideoEditing mod post: "CapCut has hit its enshittification moment. Features going behind paywall"
2. **Terms of Service are EXPLOITATIVE** — r/CapCut: ChatGPT breakdown of their ToS shows "overreaching, unfairly skewed" clauses
3. **Pricing creep** — Seedance 2.0 AI video: 90 credits for 5 seconds = expensive
4. **NOT a social media management tool** — it's a video editor that happens to export to social
5. **No scheduling** — can't post directly to platforms
6. **No content generation** — no AI writing, just video editing AI
7. **No multi-platform management** — edit → export → manually post everywhere
8. **ByteDance/TikTok concerns** — data privacy, Chinese ownership backlash

# PostForge Competitive Analysis

## The Market
Social media management + AI content generation = $20B+ market by 2027.

## Direct Competitors (AI Content Generation)

| Tool | Price | What they do | Weakness |
|------|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Predis.ai** | $32-59/mo | AI posts + images + video | Expensive, complex UI |
| **Rapidely** | $29-69/mo | AI content calendar | Pricey, targets agencies |
| **Lately.ai** | $49/mo+ | AI repurposes long content | Enterprise-focused, expensive |
| **Copy.ai** | $49/mo | General AI writing | Not social-media specific |
| **Jasper.ai** | $49-125/mo | AI writing platform | Overkill for small biz, expensive |

## Indirect Competitors (Scheduling + Some AI)

| Tool | Price | What they do | Weakness |
|------|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Buffer** | $6-12/channel | Schedule posts | No AI content generation |
| **Hootsuite** | $99-249/mo | Full social suite | Way too expensive for SMBs |
| **Later** | $25-45/mo | Visual planner | Minimal AI, Instagram-focused |
| **Planoly** | $13-23/mo | Visual planner | No AI generation |
| **SocialBee** | $29-49/mo | Scheduling + recycling | Limited AI |
| **Publer** | $12-21/mo | Multi-platform posting | Basic AI |

## PostForge Position

### We're cheaper than everyone
- Free tier: $0 (5 posts/mo)
- Pro: $9/mo (vs $29-59/mo competitors)
- Business: $19/mo (vs $49-125/mo competitors)

### Gaps we exploit
1. **Predis/Rapidely** charge $30-70/mo for AI content. We charge $9.
2. **Buffer/Later** don't generate content. They only schedule.
3. **Jasper/Copy.ai** are general-purpose. We're built specifically for SMBs.
4. **Nobody** offers industry-specific templates (contractors, salons, restaurants, etc.)
5. **Nobody** makes it dead simple — most have 50+ features. We have 3 steps.

## What we need to beat them

### Must-have (build now)
1. **AI image generation** — Predis.ai's biggest advantage. Generate post images, not just text.
2. **Direct scheduling** — Connect to Instagram/Facebook/X APIs and auto-post.
3. **Real AI** — Use actual LLM (not templates) to generate unique, non-generic content.
4. **Industry onboarding** — "I'm a contractor in Chicago" → instantly tailored content.

### Should-have (build soon)
5. **Content calendar view** — Visual drag-and-drop calendar.
6. **Analytics** — Which posts performed best, optimal posting times.
7. **Carousel/reel suggestions** — Not just static posts.
8. **Competitor monitoring** — What are your competitors posting?

### Nice-to-have (later)
9. **White-label for agencies** — Let marketing agencies rebrand PostForge.
10. **Multi-language** — Spanish, French, etc.
11. **TikTok script generator** — Hook + body + CTA format.
12. **Review response generator** — Auto-reply to Google/Yelp reviews.

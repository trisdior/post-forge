## Strategy: Land & Expand

### Phase 1: CAPTURE (Month 1-3) — We Are Here
**Goal: Get 500+ users, 100+ paying**

**Free Tier — "The Hook"**
- 10 AI posts/month (generous enough to see value)
- 1 platform
- Agent with basic memory (it learns their business)
- No auto-posting
- PostForge watermark on exports
- WHY: Let them experience the agent. Once it knows their business, switching costs are real.

**Growth Tier — $19/mo — "The No-Brainer"**
- 50 posts/month
- All platforms
- Full agent memory + learning
- Content queue + approval
- Weekly strategy reports
- WHY: $19 is impulse-buy territory. Less than lunch. Lower than EVERY competitor with AI. This is our land-grab price.

### Phase 2: MONETIZE (Month 3-6)
**Goal: 1,000+ paying users, $30K+ MRR**

Introduce higher tiers AFTER users are hooked:

**Pro Tier — $49/mo — "The Standard"**
- Unlimited posts
- Full auto-pilot mode
- Advanced agent (competitor monitoring, trend detection)
- Chat with your agent
- Analytics dashboard
- Priority generation (faster API, Sonnet instead of Haiku)
- WHY: By now they've been using the agent for months. It KNOWS them. They'll upgrade for unlimited.

**Business Tier — $99/mo — "The Agency Replacement"**
- Everything in Pro
- Multi-brand (manage 3 businesses)
- White-label exports
- API access
- Dedicated agent instance
- Phone/email support
- WHY: Agencies and multi-location businesses. High-value, low-churn.

### Phase 3: SCALE (Month 6-12)
**Goal: 2,000+ paying users, $83K+ MRR = $1M ARR**

**Agency Tier — $299/mo**
- Manage 10+ brands
- Team seats
- Client-facing dashboard
- Bulk generation
- Custom AI training on brand voice
- WHY: Marketing agencies managing multiple clients. One agency = $299/mo forever.

## Why Start at $19, Not $49

1. **We have ZERO users and ZERO social proof.** Nobody pays $49 for an unknown product.
2. **$19 is below every AI competitor.** Jasper is $49, Copy.ai is $49, Predis is $29. We undercut everyone.
3. **$19 captures market share.** We need VOLUME first. 500 users at $19 = $9,500 MRR + massive data + social proof.
4. **We can raise prices later.** Early users get $19 locked in (founding member pricing). New users pay $49 when we have traction.
5. **$19 converts from free.** The jump from $0 → $19 is easy. The jump from $0 → $49 kills conversion.
6. **YC cares about GROWTH RATE, not revenue.** 10% week-over-week growth at $19/user is better than 2% growth at $49/user.

## The Founding Member Play

**First 100 paying users get "Founding Member" pricing: $19/mo LOCKED FOR LIFE.**

This creates:
1. **Urgency** — "Only 73 spots left at $19/mo"
2. **Loyalty** — They'll never leave because they have the best deal
3. **Word of mouth** — "I'm paying $19 for what others pay $49 for"
4. **Social proof** — 100 paying users = credibility for YC application

After 100 founding members:
- New users pay $29/mo for Growth
- $49/mo for Pro
- $99/mo for Business

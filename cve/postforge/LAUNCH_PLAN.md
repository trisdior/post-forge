## Executive Summary

**PostForge** is a short-form video clipping platform that turns long-form content (podcasts, YouTube videos, training videos) into viral-optimized clips for TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, and more.

**Market Opportunity:** 33M small businesses + 500K+ creators globally need video content. Most create manually or use expensive tools. PostForge: $19-99/mo, AI-powered, one click to all platforms.

**Competitive Advantage:**
1. **Auto-post to 5 platforms** (not just clip, but publish)
2. **Analytics + Strategy** (see what works, get recommendations)
3. **Competitive intelligence** (track what others are posting)
4. **Affordable** ($19 entry vs Opus/Descript $99+)
5. **Speed** (upload → clips in 5-10 min, not manual editing)

**Revenue Model:** Freemium SaaS
- Free: 5 clips/month
- Growth: $19/mo, 50 clips
- Pro: $49/mo, 150 clips
- Business: $99/mo, 500 clips

**Year 1 Target:** 500 free users → 50 paying → $30K/mo MRR

## Product Overview

### Core Features (MVP)
1. **Upload & Clip** — AI finds viral moments automatically
2. **Smart Captions** — 4 styles (Hormozi, Minimal, Karaoke, Impact)
3. **Auto-Post** — Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook, TikTok
4. **Analytics** — Track clip performance per platform
5. **Insights** — "Which content works for YOUR audience"
6. **Competitive Tracking** — Monitor what competitors post

### What We're NOT
- Not a video editor (no timeline, effects, transitions)
- Not a creation tool (no generation of content)
- Not an influencer platform
- Not enterprise software

### What We ARE
- The easiest way to repurpose long-form into short-form
- The fastest way to post to 5 platforms simultaneously
- The smartest way to understand what works (analytics)
- The cheapest clip tool that auto-posts

- MRR (monthly recurring revenue) — target: $500 Week 2, $2K Week 4, $10K Month 2

- Platform distribution (which platforms are users posting to)

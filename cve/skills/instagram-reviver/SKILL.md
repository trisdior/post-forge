# Instagram Reviver

Bring a dead business Instagram back to life in one session.

## Process

1. **Audit current state** — Ask for Instagram handle or describe their page
2. **Collect business info:**
   - Business name and type
   - Location
   - Target audience
   - 2-3 services or products to highlight
   - Brand voice preference (casual/professional/bold/luxury)

3. **Generate revival package:**

### Bio Optimization
Write an optimized Instagram bio:
- Line 1: What you do (clear value prop)
- Line 2: Who you serve + location
- Line 3: Social proof or differentiator
- CTA: "DM 'START' for free estimate" or similar
- Relevant emoji, not excessive

### Content Calendar (30 days)
Generate 30 posts following this weekly rhythm:
- **Monday**: Tip/educational
- **Tuesday**: Behind-the-scenes or team spotlight
- **Wednesday**: Client result/showcase
- **Thursday**: Engagement post (question, poll, this-or-that)
- **Friday**: Promotional/CTA
- **Saturday**: Community/lifestyle
- **Sunday**: Testimonial or review highlight

### For each post include:
- Full caption with line breaks and emojis
- 20-30 relevant hashtags (mix of broad + niche + local)
- Image/visual description
- Best posting time
- Content type label

### Hashtag Strategy
Create 3 hashtag sets to rotate:
- **Set A**: Industry-focused (15 tags)
- **Set B**: Local/community (15 tags)
- **Set C**: Engagement/growth (15 tags)

### Posting Schedule
Recommend optimal times based on industry:
- Service businesses: Tue-Thu 9-11am, Sat 10am
- Restaurants: Wed-Fri 11am-1pm, Sat-Sun 5-7pm
- Retail: Mon/Wed/Fri 12pm, Sat 10am

## Output Format
Deliver as structured, copy-paste-ready content. Each post should be immediately usable — no placeholder text, no "[insert here]" fields.

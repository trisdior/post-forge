# Social Content Generator

Generate a full month of social media content tailored to any business.

## Usage

When triggered, collect:
1. **Business name** and type
2. **Target platform(s)** (Instagram, Facebook, X, LinkedIn, TikTok)
3. **Location** (for local businesses)
4. **Brand voice** (professional, casual, bold, friendly)
5. **Number of posts** (default: 30)

## Content Mix (per 30 posts)

- **8 tips/educational** — Position as expert, provide value
- **6 showcase/portfolio** — Show work, before/after, results
- **6 engagement** — Questions, polls, "which do you prefer?"
- **4 testimonials** — Client reviews, success stories
- **3 promotional** — Offers, CTAs, book now
- **3 community** — Behind the scenes, team, local events

## Output Format

For each post, generate:
```
Post #[N] — [Date] ([Day])
Platform: [platform]
Type: [tip/showcase/engagement/testimonial/promo/community]

[Full post content with emojis, line breaks, hashtags]

Image suggestion: [Description of ideal image to pair]
```

## Platform Rules

- **Instagram**: 2,200 char max. 20-30 hashtags. Visual-first. Use line breaks.
- **Facebook**: Longer copy OK. 1-3 hashtags max. Conversational. Include CTA.
- **X/Twitter**: 280 char max. 1-3 hashtags. Punchy. Thread for longer content.
- **LinkedIn**: Professional tone. 1-5 hashtags. Story-driven.
- **TikTok**: Hook in first line. Trending sounds reference. 3-5 hashtags.

## Industry Templates

Adapt content for: contractors, realtors, restaurants, salons, fitness, law firms, dentists, auto shops, e-commerce, coaches, photographers, agencies.

## Example

User: "Generate 30 days of Instagram content for my painting business in Denver"

Generate 30 posts following the content mix, with painting-specific tips, project showcases, seasonal promotions (spring = exterior painting season), and local Denver references.

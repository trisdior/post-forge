## 📱 Platform-Specific Tips

### Twitter Strategy
- Post 9am CST (optimal engagement time)
- Use 2-3 hashtags max (#AI, #Business, #Entrepreneurship)
- Thread format: 8-10 tweets, space them out
- Quote tweet Day 1 when posting Day 2 (thread continuity)
- Engage with replies within 1 hour (boost algorithm)
- Retweet early supporters (build community)

### Substack Strategy
- Email subject line is key (gets your open rate)
- Include call-to-action at end (reply, share, etc)
- Format: Story → Insight → Action
- Email after 5pm CST (higher evening open rate)
- Add post-email in browser too (discoverable)
- Build archive (back-issues increase trust)

### YouTube Strategy
- Upload at 8pm CST (watch time boost next day)
- Titles: Hook question + promise ("Why This Fails: [Concept]")
- Thumbnails: High contrast, emotional face, text overlay
- First 30 seconds: Hook (why you should watch)
- Cards at min 2:00 (link to next video)
- Playlist: Link all 5 together

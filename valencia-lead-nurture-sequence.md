# Valencia Construction Lead Nurture Sequence

**Goal:** Turn cold leads into consultations within 7-14 days. Get that first paying client.

**Channels:** SMS (immediate), Facebook Messenger (conversion), Email (long-term follow-up)

---

## Segment: Homeowners Interested in Kitchen/Bathroom Remodels

**Entry Point:** Form submission on website OR Facebook lead form

---

## SMS Sequence (Immediate - Day 1)

### Text 1 (Within 5 Minutes)
**Send:** After form submission, instant auto-response

```
Hi [Name]! 👋 

Thanks for reaching out to Valencia Construction. 

I'm Tris (the owner). We specialize in kitchen & bathroom remodels in Chicago.

Quick question: Are you looking for a timeline, or do you have a rough budget in mind?

Reply with "Timeline" or "Budget" so I can help you better 👍
```

**Goal:** Immediate engagement. Show owner cares.

---

### Text 2 (Day 1 - 6 Hours Later)
**Trigger:** If no reply to Text 1, send value-add

```
No rush on the timing question 😊

While you think about it, here's something useful:

Most kitchen remodels in Chicago run $20-50K. Bathrooms $15-30K.

The difference comes down to: finishes, scope, timeline.

Want me to send a quick breakdown of what goes into pricing?

Y / N
```

**Goal:** Provide value. Lower perceived risk.

---

**Trigger:** Based on user response

**If Y (wants breakdown):**
```

- Budget ($20-30K): New cabinets, countertops, appliances, paint
```

**If N (doesn't want breakdown):**
```

Talk soon!
```

**Goal:** Provide specific value OR stay in contact.

## Why LinkedIn for a Local Contractor?

LinkedIn is where **decision-makers** live:
- **Property managers** (apartment buildings, complexes)
- **Real estate investors** (flipping homes, rental properties)
- **General contractors** (potential partners, referrals)
- **Interior designers** (they need reliable GCs for projects)
- **Real estate agents** (they recommend contractors to clients)
- **Facilities managers** (commercial spaces, office builds)

**The play:** Position Valencia as the reliable, professional contractor that decision-makers trust.

LinkedIn engagement is 5x higher than Facebook for B2B. One post to a property manager could be worth $50K+ in future work.

## Content Strategy

### Posting Frequency
- **Goal:** 2-3 posts per week
- **Best days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday (9-10 AM Chicago time)
- **Best time:** Weekday mornings (people check LinkedIn at their desk)

### Content Pillars (What to Post)

#### Pillar 1: Project Spotlights (40% of posts)
Show completed work. Build credibility and inspire.
- Before/after project highlights
- Materials used, timeline, complexity
- Keep it professional, not flashy

#### Pillar 2: Industry Insights (30% of posts)
Position as expert. Attract decision-makers looking for knowledge.
- Chicago real estate market trends
- When to remodel vs. upgrade
- ROI of home improvements
- Contractor tips for property managers
- Building code updates (if relevant)

#### Pillar 3: Behind-the-Scenes (20% of posts)
Build relationship and trust.
- Team photos
- Day-in-the-life content
- How you approach a project
- Quality standards and process

#### Pillar 4: Social Proof (10% of posts)
Testimonials, awards, certifications.
- Client testimonials (repurposed from Google reviews)
- Industry certifications (if any)
- Awards or recognition
- Milestones (first client, 10 projects, etc.)

## How to Schedule Posts on LinkedIn

1. Write post (in draft or document)
2. Go to Valencia company page
3. Click **Compose** (or **Create**)
4. Paste content
5. Add image/video if you have it
6. Click **Schedule** (bottom left)
7. Pick date + time (Tuesday-Thursday, 9-10 AM)
8. Confirm

Terms & Conditions
  1. Scope of Work: This proposal covers only the work explicitly described above. Any additional work requested will require a written change order with agreed-upon pricing before work begins.
  2. Change Orders: Changes to the scope of work must be documented in writing and signed by both parties. Additional costs and timeline adjustments will be outlined in the change order.
  3. Permits & Inspections: Valencia Construction will obtain all necessary permits required by the City of Chicago. Permit fees are included in the proposal price unless otherwise noted. We coordinate all required inspections.
  4. Materials: All materials are as specified in the scope of work. Substitutions due to availability will be discussed with the client and approved before installation. Upgraded materials requested by the client may result in additional cost.
  5. Payment Terms: Payments are due as outlined in the payment schedule above. Payments not received within 7 days of the milestone may result in work stoppage. We accept check, cash, Zelle, and bank transfer.
  6. Timeline: Estimated timelines are provided in good faith. Delays due to permit processing, material backorders, weather, or client-requested changes are not the responsibility of Valencia Construction. We will communicate any delays promptly.
  7. Warranty: Valencia Construction warrants all labor for a period of one (1) year from the date of project completion. Manufacturer warranties on materials and fixtures apply separately and will be provided to the client.
  8. Insurance: Valencia Construction carries general liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage. Certificates of insurance are available upon request.
  9. Access: Client agrees to provide reasonable access to the project site during working hours (typically 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM, Monday – Saturday). Restricted access may affect the project timeline.
  10. Client Responsibilities: Client is responsible for removing personal belongings from the work area prior to project start. Valencia Construction is not responsible for items left in the work zone.
  11. Cancellation: Either party may cancel this agreement with written notice. If the client cancels after work has begun, client is responsible for payment of all work completed and materials ordered to date.
  12. Dispute Resolution: Any disputes arising from this agreement will be resolved through mediation in Cook County, Illinois before any legal action is taken.
  13. Proposal Validity: This proposal is valid for 30 days from the date issued. After 30 days, pricing and availability may change.

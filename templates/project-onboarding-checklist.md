## ✅ Phase 1: Post-Signature (Within 24 Hours)

### Legal & Administrative
- [ ] Signed contract received and filed
- [ ] Payment method verified and payment schedule documented
- [ ] Client liability insurance verified (if required by contract)
- [ ] Permit requirements identified and responsibility assigned
- [ ] Workers' comp insurance verified for all subcontractors
- [ ] Lien waiver forms prepared for client
- [ ] Change order template printed and available

### Client Communication
- [ ] Welcome email sent with project timeline and expectations
- [ ] PM contact info provided to client (phone + email)
- [ ] Emergency contact number established
- [ ] First site meeting scheduled (within 3-5 business days)
- [ ] Client expectations documented in writing
- [ ] Payment schedule calendar sent to client
- [ ] Parking/access instructions clarified

### Project Setup
- [ ] Project folder created (physical + digital)
- [ ] Job costing spreadsheet started with budget breakdown
- [ ] Subcontractor list identified and quotes obtained
- [ ] Material supplier list and pricing locked
- [ ] Project timeline created with milestone dates
- [ ] Site conditions photos taken (baseline)
- [ ] Utility locates requested (call 811 if needed)

---

## ✅ Phase 2: Pre-Start Meeting (3-5 Days Before Work Begins)

### On-Site Walkthrough
- [ ] Client present for full project review
- [ ] Scope of work reviewed line-by-line
- [ ] Project timeline and phases explained
- [ ] Site access hours established
- [ ] Parking arrangements confirmed
- [ ] Utility shutoffs/bypasses explained
- [ ] Dust/noise management plan discussed
- [ ] Neighbor notification plan confirmed (if needed)

### Logistics Clarity
- [ ] Material delivery schedule confirmed with suppliers
- [ ] Dumpster/waste removal date confirmed
- [ ] Tools and equipment staging area identified
- [ ] Safety hazards identified and mitigation plan discussed
- [ ] Existing structure conditions documented (photos + written notes)
- [ ] Finish selections confirmed (colors, styles, materials)
- [ ] Client authorization for on-site decisions established

### Team Briefing
- [ ] All crew members briefed on scope and timeline
- [ ] Safety requirements communicated
- [ ] Client communication protocol established
- [ ] Quality standards and tolerances explained
- [ ] Site cleanliness expectations set

---

## ✅ Phase 3: Day 1 of Work

### Crew Readiness
- [ ] All tools present and functional
- [ ] Materials delivered and inspected
- [ ] Safety equipment available (PPE, first aid, etc.)
- [ ] Work schedule posted at site
- [ ] Client contact info available to crew

### Client Handoff
- [ ] Client and PM review site setup together
- [ ] Daily communication expectations set (best time to call, check-ins)
- [ ] Progress photo protocol established
- [ ] Punch list process explained
- [ ] Daily/weekly site closure procedures reviewed

### Documentation
- [ ] Daily log started (time, crew, activities, weather)
- [ ] Time tracking system ready
- [ ] Photo documentation protocol in place
- [ ] Baseline conditions confirmed (before photos)

---

## ✅ Phase 4: Weekly Touchpoints (During Project)

### Client Communication (Every Monday or As Scheduled)
- [ ] Weekly progress update email/call scheduled with client
- [ ] Progress photos sent (at least 3-5 per week)
- [ ] Timeline status communicated (on track / at risk)
- [ ] Any issues or delays explained with mitigation plan
- [ ] Next week's plan shared
- [ ] Budget status reviewed (if applicable)
- [ ] Questions/change requests addressed within 24 hours

### Project Management
- [ ] Weekly crew coordination meeting held
- [ ] Material deliveries on schedule
- [ ] Labor hours logged and reconciled
- [ ] Quality inspections performed daily
- [ ] Safety incidents logged (if any)
- [ ] Budget tracking updated
- [ ] Subcontractor invoices received and cross-checked

---

## ✅ Phase 5: Final Inspection & Closeout

### Quality Control
- [ ] Walk final inspection with crew (punch list creation)
- [ ] All punch list items documented with photos
- [ ] Client walk-through scheduled (client + PM + key crew)
- [ ] Punch list reviewed with client (written)
- [ ] Client sign-off on completed work obtained in writing
- [ ] Any remaining issues resolved or scheduled

### Final Documentation
- [ ] All invoices collected and paid
- [ ] Time logs finalized and archived
- [ ] All change orders documented and reconciled
- [ ] Warranty documents compiled
- [ ] Care & maintenance instructions provided to client
- [ ] Product manuals and warranties handed over
- [ ] Before/after photo set organized and labeled

### Payment & Closeout
- [ ] Final payment invoice issued
- [ ] Payment received and reconciled
- [ ] Lien waiver signed and filed (if required by contract)
- [ ] All crew and subcontractor invoices paid
- [ ] Job cost analysis completed (actual vs. budget)
- [ ] Site fully cleaned and debris removed
- [ ] Client satisfaction survey/feedback request sent

### Testimonial & Marketing
- [ ] Testimonial/review request sent to client (email + text option)
- [ ] Before/after photos tagged for website/marketing use
- [ ] Case study potential evaluated (ask permission to feature project)
- [ ] Client provided with referral incentive details

---

## ✅ Phase 6: Post-Project (30-90 Days)

### Follow-Up & Relationship Building
- [ ] 30-day follow-up call to check satisfaction
- [ ] Any warranty issues addressed immediately
- [ ] Referral thank-you sent (if they refer business)
- [ ] Client invited to attend project gallery event (if applicable)
- [ ] Contact details updated for future marketing

### Learning & Improvement
- [ ] Project profitability analyzed
- [ ] Team feedback collected on scope, timeline, coordination
- [ ] What went well documented for future projects
- [ ] What to improve documented (process notes)
- [ ] Budget variance analyzed and notes added to job file

---

## 📝 Key Documents to Have Ready

Before any project starts, ensure these are ready to go:

- ✅ Signed contract (reviewed and filed)
- ✅ Detailed scope of work (line-item breakdown)
- ✅ Budget/pricing breakdown
- ✅ Project timeline (milestone dates)
- ✅ Change order template (signed)
- ✅ Lien waiver forms (if in IL)
- ✅ Payment schedule calendar
- ✅ Insurance verification documents
- ✅ Site safety plan (if required)
- ✅ Subcontractor agreements and insurance
- ✅ Material specifications and quotes

## 🎯 Success Metrics

A project is "onboarded well" when:
- ✅ Client feels informed and confident
- ✅ Team knows exactly what to do
- ✅ No miscommunications about scope, timeline, or cost
- ✅ Site is organized and safe
- ✅ Budget tracking is accurate from day 1
- ✅ Client is checking in positively weekly
- ✅ Project finishes on time and on budget
- ✅ Client refers Valencia to friends/family
